SUMMARY: BUSINESS VALUE METRICS FRAMEWORK

1. **Financial Metrics**
   - IT Cost per Employee: Monthly IT spend / Employee count
   - ROI on Digital Initiatives: (Benefits - Costs) / Costs * 100
   - Downtime Cost Impact: Hours * Revenue per Hour
   - Cloud Migration Savings: Legacy costs - Cloud costs

2. **Productivity Metrics** 
   - Digital Workplace Adoption: Modern tools usage %
   - Employee Digital Score: Productive vs non-productive time
   - Remote Work Effectiveness: VPN usage correlation
   - Automation Ratio: Automated vs manual operations %

3. **Strategic Metrics**
   - Digital Transformation Progress: Cloud adoption rate
   - Innovation Index: New technology adoption speed  
   - Agility Score: Time to deploy new services
   - Competitive Advantage: Technology capability vs industry

4. **Risk & Compliance Metrics**
   - Security Posture Score: Threats detected vs mitigated
   - Compliance Score: Audit findings / total checks * 100
   - Business Continuity: RTO/RPO achievement rate
   - Data Protection: GDPR compliance percentage
